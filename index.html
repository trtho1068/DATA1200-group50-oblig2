Nonsense thoughts

A photography and some thoughts

Authors:

Eriksen, Sigurd Sunde; Miras, Stella Midsian; Nerem, Vilde Elise Krogsæter; Thorsteinsson, Trond

Writen the 23th of september 2023

Photographer: Sigurd

This is a picture was photographed in Innlandet county in Norway. One thing that immediately becomes clear when one observes this picture is that the picture was in fact not captured during winter. We are certain that this statement in fact is true due to the green vegetation and the lack of snow. Three colours dominate the template of this pictures, these colours are blue, white and green, but we can also observe elements of grey, brown and even tones of yellow.

The meaning behind the picture

If we were to summarize the essential themes in this picture it would be potential, growth and life, but also doom, which is inherent to al life. We start by looking at the picture from the left, and what do we see? An open field, ripe for opportunity and potential, and in the background, we see great hills symbolizing the great potential of the future. As we move our eyes further to the right, we observe that the vegetation starts to grow, and soon enough they have become threes. Threes that represent the life and al its greatness. At the same time, we observe that the hills in the background steadily becomes smaller, this fall in hight represents how all life one day must fall, and that nothing can last forever. Lastly, we observe a path that leads to the right, which symbolizes the path into the future. This path leads out of our field of view representing how the future is unknown, further strengthening the theme of life and the theme of potential.
